Benefits of green apples

Rich in Vitamins and Minerals:Green apples are rich in vitamins like vitamin C and vitamin A, as well as minerals like potassium and calcium. Aid in Weight Loss: Due to their high fiber and water content, green apples are an excellent choice for those looking to lose weight. They help in keeping you feeling full for longer periods without adding excess calories.
